This is a booklet that helps you reflect on the past year and plan the next one. With carefully selected questions and exercises, YearCompass helps you uncover your patterns and design a great year for yourself.

  • First half: Review, learn from, and celebrate the year behind
  • Second half: Dream, plan, and prepare for the year ahead
  • Time needed: A few hours of uninterrupted focus
  • Tools needed: An open and honest mind

Can you do this in a group?

Sure! Grab your friends and find a comfortable space. Everyone fills out their own YearCompass, but you can take breaks to discuss and share.
